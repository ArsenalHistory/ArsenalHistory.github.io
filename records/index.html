How many times have Arsenal won the league?

Arsenal have won the league title 18 times which is the third highest in history.

Arsenal won the league title in the following years: 1930-31, 1932-33, 1933-34, 1934-35, 1937-38, 1947-48, 1952-53, 1970-71, 1988-89, 1990-91, 1997-98, 2001-02, 2003-04

How many times have Arsenal won the FA Cup?

Arsenal have won the FA Cup an unprecedented 14 times. The most recent came in 2020 when Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang double helped Arsenal overcome Chelsea 2-1.

What is Arsenal's longest unbeaten run in the league?

Arsenal went unbeaten for 49 games from 2003 to 2004. This is the longest an english side has gone unbeaten in the history of league football. Arsenal were crowned "The Invincibles" as a result of the feat
